How scope works

The right package is confirmed before the quote is finalized.

  • Prices are starting points.
  • Final quote is confirmed after the discovery call.
  • Revision rounds vary by package.
  • Additional revisions are billed separately.
  • Travel and special costs are quoted before approval.
  • Client handles permissions for locations, people, logos, and third-party assets they provide.
  • Final delivered assets include organic promotional usage rights for client-owned web, social, email, pitch, and presentation use.
  • Paid ads, broadcast placement, sublicensing, or third-party distribution can be scoped separately.
  • Raw footage and project files are not included unless specifically quoted.
  • AI, animation, or character work is scoped before production.
